Use Apartments.com for all non-emergency maintenance requests.

**Link:** [Submit a Maintenance Request](#)

## Include the Following in Your Request

- The room or area
- A clear description of the issue
- When the issue started
- Photos, if available
- Whether pets are in the unit
- Whether we may enter if you are not home

## For Emergencies

Call 911 for any immediate safety risk, then follow the emergency contact instructions in your lease.

Examples of emergency conditions:

- Fire or smoke
- Gas odor
- Active flooding
- Medical emergency
- Any immediate danger to people or property
