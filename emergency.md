## Call 911 For

- Fire
- Smoke
- Gas odor
- Active flooding
- Medical emergency
- Any immediate safety risk

After calling 911, notify us through the emergency contact method listed in your lease.

## Local Non-Emergency Contacts

- **Lower Merion Police Non-Emergency:** [610-649-1000](tel:610-649-1000)
- **Gladwyne Fire Company:** [610-642-9586](tel:610-642-9586)

## Property Emergencies

For urgent property issues that are not life-threatening, use the emergency contact method listed in your lease after taking any needed immediate safety steps.
