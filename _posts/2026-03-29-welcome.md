Welcome to your new apartment.

We want your move-in to feel clear, simple, and well organized from day one. The first few days are usually about getting settled, confirming everything is working correctly, and documenting anything that needs attention. After that, the focus shifts to learning the space, staying on top of small issues early, and building good communication habits with management.

To make that easier, we put together two short guides for you:

- [Your First Week - yppmanagement.com/first-week](https://yppmanagement.com/first-week)

  What to do before move-in, on move-in day, and during your first week in the apartment.

- [Your First 90 Days - yppmanagement.com/first-90](https://yppmanagement.com/first-90)

  What to expect as you settle in, how to handle normal wear in an older home, and how to report real issues early.

- Direct contact to your property coordinator

  Any issues challenges or questions can be made directed to Dave at [267-551-0970](tel:267-551-0970). Text messages preferred for non-emergency communication. You can expect a response within 24 hours.

If you follow those two guides, you will avoid most common move-in problems and have a much smoother start in your new home.

## A few important reminders

- Report any condition issues in writing as early as possible after move-in
- Keep utilities, insurance, and payments active and current
- Do not make repairs or alterations without approval
- Send maintenance requests clearly: what happened, where, and when you noticed it
