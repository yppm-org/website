This portal is here to help you manage the basic parts of your tenancy in one place.

You can use this site to:

- Access Apartments.com for rent payments
- Submit non-emergency maintenance requests
- Review utility setup steps
- Find local trash and recycling information
- Locate emergency and non-emergency contacts
- Review move-in information

## How to Use This Portal

Use Apartments.com for:

- Rent payments
- Auto Pay setup
- Maintenance requests
- Routine account activity

Use this portal for:

- Service setup information
- Local phone numbers
- Township trash and recycling resources
- Property guidance and reminders

## Important Service Information

The following services require tenant accounts:

- PECO electric
- Comcast, if chosen
- Verizon, if chosen

The following services are included in rent and do not require tenant accounts:

- Water
- Sewer
- Trash
- Recycling
