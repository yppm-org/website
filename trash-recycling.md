Trash, sewer, and recycling service are included in your rent.

No separate account is needed for these services.

## Please Review Local Rules Before Pickup

Helpful topics include:

- Trash pickup schedule
- Recycling schedule
- What can be recycled
- What cannot be recycled
- Holiday pickup changes
- Bulk item disposal
- Hazardous waste disposal
