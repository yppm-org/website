## Electric Service

PECO provides electric service to the property.

Tenants must create a PECO account for service at their unit.

**Link:** [Set Up PECO Service](#)

## Internet and Cable

Both Comcast and Verizon serve the property.

Tenants must create an account directly with the provider they choose.

**Links:**

- [Set Up Comcast Service](#)
- [Set Up Verizon Service](#)

## Included in Rent

The following services are included in rent. No tenant account is needed for these:

- Water
- Sewer
- Trash
- Recycling
